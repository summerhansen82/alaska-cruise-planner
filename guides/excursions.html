Excursions & Ports

Alaska ports are all about choosing the right mix of adventure, scenery, and budget. Here are some common options.

Popular Excursion Types

  • Glacier helicopter landings
  • Whale-watching and wildlife boat tours
  • Dog sledding experiences (on snow or on dry land)
  • Guided city walking tours with local history
  • Self-guided exploring with a day pass for buses or shuttles

Sample Port Ideas

  • Juneau: Mendenhall Glacier, whale-watching, Mount Roberts Tramway
  • Skagway: White Pass & Yukon Route railway, easy town walk, hiking trails
  • Ketchikan: Creek Street boardwalk, totem parks, seafood and shops
  • Sitka: Raptor center, fortress of the bear, coastal walks

Quick Planning Tips

  1. Decide your budget per port before you browse excursions.
  2. Pick one “big” excursion and keep the others low-key.
  3. Leave at least one port mostly open for wandering and shopping.
